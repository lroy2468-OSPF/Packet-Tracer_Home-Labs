**VLAN + OSPF**



**## Overview ##**



This lab demonstrates

* OSPF
* Verification
* Vlan configuration



**## Topology ##**



\- Lab 1 -

* 2 Routers
* 2 Switches
* 2 PCs



\- IP Addressing -

* VLAN 10: 192.168.10.0/30
* Gateway: 192.168.10.1



* VLAN 20: 192.168.20.0/30
* Gateway: 192.168.20.1



* P2P Link: 10.0.0.0/30



**## Notes \& Skills Demonstrated ##**



* Subnetting
* Vlan creation
* Verification
* OSPF routing
* Documentation
* Vlans configured on switch, switchports set, sub-interfaces on router, PCs configured and finally OSPF initialized with Vlans and router ID.
